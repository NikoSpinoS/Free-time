Eve Online: Ways to make ISK for newbros

  • Ratting

    Ratting is based on the budget of the ship that you're flying. If your
    ship is rational for the rats that you are about to engage, then you won't
    have any problem. Drone ships are very recommended for ratting, as they
    can kite and be unable to get hit. Gila is a very good cruiser for ratting with
    its huge drone damage and hitpoints bonuses. It's not that recommended for
    new players as it cost about 200 million ISK but it's worth buying.

  • Exploration

    Exploration is as risky as an effective way to make ISK.
    If you are a new player and interested on exploration, there are some tier-1
    frigates that are used for exploration, like: Heron, Imicus, Probe and Magnate.
    With a little attention to dscan and local, they are good for doing data and relic sites.
    Unfortunately, only omegas can fit a cloaking device, that's the reason that makes it risky.

  • Mining

    The safest way to make ISK, is mining.
    If you recently joined New Eden, train some industry core skills, take your
    Venture and start mining some asteroid belts. The ore that you
    mined, you can sell it, or reprocess it and gain materials that are used for
    crafting ships, modules and everything that you need. Ore's value depends on the
    system's security, which means that low security systems contain more expensive ore.
